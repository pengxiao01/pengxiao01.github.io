Current Research

Life Cycle Assessment of Water Resource Recovery Systems

Keywords: LCA, TEA, Wastewater Modeling

Conduct comprehensive LCA and techno-economic analysis for novel wastewater resource recovery systems. This interdisciplinary project involves developing models for intelligent water treatment technologies with focus on sustainability and economic viability.

Published in Nature Water

Environmental Systems Modeling

Keywords: Machine Learning, Network analysis, Time Series

Develop hybrid models combining process-based and data-driven approaches for complex water systems, including prediction of contaminant removal and wastewater flow forecasting..

Featured on Journal Cover

Intelligent Environmental Decision Support

Keywords: Climate Change Adapation, Electric Vehicle/Machinery, Mining

Develop frameworks integrating climate projections, historical data, and regional infrastructure characteristics to forecast demand, greenhouse gas emissions, and energy consumption. The tool provides specific recommendations by analyzing trade-offs between electrification strategies, climate vulnerabilities, and water management, helping stakeholders prioritize adaptation strategies and infrastructure investments.
